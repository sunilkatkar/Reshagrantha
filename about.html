Our Story

The firm has undertaken a wide spectrum of projects ranging from conservation, residential, commercial, interiors, artwork, and landscape. Reshagrantha is a Creative and outstanding national award-winning firm of 2022, and the Principal architect is awarded as the most promising and trendsetter architect of India 2022. Reshagrantha is uncompromising concerning quality, perfection & excellence. We focus on developing long-term client relationships through proven execution. With nature-oriented work ethics, we develop a Sui generis personality.

As an architect & designer, it becomes an utmost duty to design & cater to the needs of wild animals & nature.
